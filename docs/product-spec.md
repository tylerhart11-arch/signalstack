# Product Spec

## Goal

SignalStack is a personal market-intelligence terminal for top-down investors. The MVP focuses on four workflows:

1. Scan the market quickly.
2. Understand the current macro regime.
3. Investigate unusual cross-asset behavior.
4. Translate a thesis into possible exposures and watch signals.

## Core Sections

### Overview

- Track major cross-asset indicators across equities, rates, credit, FX, commodities, inflation, labor, and policy.
- Show latest level, recent move, 1M and 3M direction, sparkline, and interpretation label.

### Regime

- Score observable macro pillars with transparent logic.
- Classify into a single current regime with confidence, top drivers, alternate scores, and regime history.

### Curiosity Feed

- Surface rule-based anomalies where cross-asset confirmation breaks down.
- Rank by severity, divergence, novelty, and breadth.

### Thesis Builder

- Convert plain-English investment ideas into a structured exposure map.
- Return theme family, time horizon, sectors, ETFs, stocks, confirming signals, and invalidation risks.

## Product Principles

- Local-first and single-user
- Transparent logic over black-box sophistication
- Useful even in demo mode
- Terminal-inspired, but readable and professional
- Easy to extend into watchlists, alerts, and portfolio features later
